Powerhouse
Take your Minecraft world to the next level with god-tier gear and items. This mod adds ultra-powerful items designed for players who want to dominate their environment.

What’s Inside?
Insane Tools: Mining and combat tools that break blocks instantly and deal massive damage.

Super Potions: A new potion that can make you invincible.

Magic Liquids: New fluids with unique properties—Pusher and Puller, its self-explanatory.
